## The Business
**MarineGuard AI** — Automates crew change documentation for ship management companies.

Problem: Manual crew change docs cause $10K-$100K/day delays. Crewing managers
juggle 20+ vessels on Excel and WhatsApp. One missing cert = vessel off-hire.

Product: AI generates document checklists per seafarer, tracks expiry dates,
flags missing items — before the crew change, not during.

NOT safety-critical: Crew change docs are administrative. Errors are catchable
by a human before they cause harm. This is intentional — no liability wall.

Anti-hallucination rule: NEVER invent certificate requirements or regulatory
citations. Always cite source convention or flag circular. Always include
disclaimer on outputs. Human reviews every output before operational use.
